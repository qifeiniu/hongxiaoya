## Design Context

### Users
Young adults looking for dating and social networking ("红小鸭相亲交友"). They want a modern, trendy, and friendly platform to meet new people.

### Brand Personality
Fresh, breathable, young, energetic, rounded, and approachable.
3 words: Young, Energetic, Approachable.

### Aesthetic Direction
- **Clear and Breathable**: Large amounts of white space and very light gray-purple backgrounds.
- **Young and Energetic**: High saturation, high brightness "Indigo Purple" (`#6A61F8`) as the main brand color, with "Coral Pink" (`#FF5E7D`) for emotional/heartbeat interactions.
- **Rounded and Approachable**: Extensive use of large border radii (cards 32rpx, pill buttons 100rpx) to eliminate sharpness.
- **Shadows**: Soft, diffused shadows and "breathing glow" effects for buttons, instead of harsh black shadows.

### Design Principles
1. **Strictly adhere to the Color System**: Use the defined brand colors (`#6A61F8`, `#FF5E7D`), neutral text colors (`#1A1A24`, `#4A4A5A`), and backgrounds (`#F7F8FA` for page, `#FFFFFF` for cards). Never use pure black `#000000`.
2. **Rounded corners everywhere**: Cards use 32rpx, base buttons/inputs 16rpx, primary action buttons/pills 100rpx.
3. **Soft, colored shadows**: Use diffused shadows (`rgba(0,0,0,0.04)`) for cards and colored glows for primary/accent buttons (`rgba(106, 97, 248, 0.25)`).
4. **Use SCSS Variables**: Always use the global SCSS variables defined in `uni.scss`.
